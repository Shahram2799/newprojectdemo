Achievements and Trophies

The following achievements and trophies can be achieved during one or multiple playthroughs of the game. These are discussed in a separate section as these are may or may not naturally happen during first playthrough of the game. Do note that trophies for Multiplayer will not be covered as this guide is more single player story mode focused, but the multiplayer trophies are easy to understand as well as they are pretty self explanatory.

Solid Gold, Baby!

You earned 70 Gold Medals on Missions and Strangers and Freaks.

This trophy can be naturally unlocked as you keep fulfilling gold medal objectives for story missions and strangers and freaks missions. After completing a mission, you can also replay that mission through the pause menu. Replay and go for the Gold Medal Objectives if you haven't got one already.

San Andreas Sightseer

You explored all of Los Santos and Blaine County.

This trophy can be naturally unlocked as you keep exploring the entire map of San Andreas by completing story related missions, non story related, activities and collectibles. To explore the map faster and unlock the trophy, you can grab a helicopter from any airfield you can find and fly around the whole map as fast as you can.

Three Man Army

UNDER THE BRIDGE CHALLENGES

You can fly under a total of 50 bridges all around the state of San Andreas with a plane or a helicopter. This challenge can be performed by any of the protagonist and is an easy and fast way to increase the Flying Ability stat. Although there is a total of 50 bridges to fly under for the trophy, you only need to complete 25 for 100% completion. You can find a buzzard to easily complete this challenge either by purchasing one or finding one on the roof of the NOOSE Headquarters in Palomino Highlands.

Video Walkthrough

KNIFE FLIGHTS CHALLENGES

You can perform a total of 15 knife flights between buildings all around the state of San Andreas with a plane. To perform a knife flight, you have to turn a plane sideways while you are flying. This challenge can be performed by any of the protagonist and is an easy and fast way to increase the Flying Ability stat. Although there is a total of 15 bridges to fly under for the trophy, you only need to complete 8 for 100% completion. You can find a plane to easily complete this challenge either by purchasing one or finding one at LSIA or a different hangar.

STUNT JUMPS

Taking from previous GTA titles, you can perform a total of 50 Stunt Jumps all around the state of San Andreas with a car or a bike. This challenge can be performed by any of the protagonist, but it is easier to use Franklin for his special ability. Although there is a total of 50 stunt jumps for the trophy, you only need to complete 25 for 100% completion.
