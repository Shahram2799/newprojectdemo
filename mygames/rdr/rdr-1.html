Bounties

In RDR I the bounty hunting side mission requires you to find a wanted poster and deliver the outlaw, dead or alive, to the nearest prison for monetary rewards. You can find Wanted Posters placed on walls near Train Stations and Prisons inside a settlement by a Marshall. There are 45 bounties total, but you only need to find at least 1 bounty in all 20 possible locations they could spawn in for 100% Completion. You can track the number of bounty locations you have completed under Stats > Score in the Pause Menu.

All Bounties
Do note that when you view a wanted poster, you can see where to find the bounty from one of the 20 possible locations the game assigns.
It is recommended to capture a bounty alive as they have far more value being alive than dead.
Game Progression is tracked by the location of the bounties and not the bounties themselves, so you don't need to get every bounty. You only need to find and get 1 bounty each at 20 different locations for 100% Completion.

Jobs

In RDR I you can participate in horse breaking and nightwatch jobs for monetary rewards. The Horsebreaking side mission requires you to break a horse inside a corral and return it to the rancher. The Nightwatch Job side mission requires you to protect a settlement from rustlers and bandits while on night patrol with a watchdog. Completing all Horsebreaking and Nightwatch jobs is needed for 100% Completion. You can track the jobs you completed via Stats > General in the Pause Menu.

There are 16 missing persons total, and you need to find and rescue all of them for 100% Completion.

Undead Nightmare - Miscellaneous

In addition to 100% completion for Undead Nightmare, there are also two miscellaneous tasks that mostly would be naturally completed as you complete the story and go for 100% Completion. These miscellaneous tasks are all required for 100% Completion.

UNICORN

In Undead Nightmare, after reaching Rank 5 in all of the Undead Hunter Challenges and the Four Horses of the Apocalypse Challenges, you can find a Mytical Creature around Torquemada. There is a Unicorn roaming about with rainbow highlights at the tail that you can break and own. Finding and breaking the Mythical Unicorn is needed for 100% Completion.

Fan Service



Outfits

In Undead Nightmare you can wear 11 different outfits to change John's style at a safehouse in a saved town. Some outfits are given automatically or awarded for completing missions, while other outfits must be earned. You can earn outfits by finding and collecting different outfit scraps for that outfit by completing various jobs. Although there are 10 outfits total, you only need to earn 1 specific outfit for 100% Completion (funnily enough, you will naturally earn this outfit as you go towards 100% Completion). This outfit is marked with a colored bar on the left. You can track how many outfits you have discovered and completed via Stats > Misc in the pause menu.
