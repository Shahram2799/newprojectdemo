Progress Tracker

To complete 100% Full Synchronization with Assassin's Creed Unity, you have to complete all Single Player Missions, Co-op Missions, Paris Stories, and Miscellaneous Tasks. You can use the Progress Tracker in the pause menu to track how much progress you have completed towards 100% Synchronization. It is recommended to complete secondary missions after completion of the main storyline as some features for 100% sync are locked until you complete a specific sequence. It is also recommended to synchronize with all viewpoints as early as possible to make traveling easier and also unlock all skills and weapons as early as you can to upgrade Arno all the way to Level 5 to make the missions easier.

Achievements and Trophies

The following achievements and trophies can be achieved during one or multiple playthroughs of the game, or in free roam after Story Completion. These are discussed in a separate section as these are may or may not naturally happen during first playthrough of the game. Trophies and Achievements can also be obtained during free roam after completion of the main game.

Thawed

Free a total of 10 trapped Assassins.

To get this trophy, you can free at least 10 trapped assassins in different helix rift missions as part of the objectives. You will naturally free at least 8 assassins through playing each helix rift mission at least once for total 100% sync. Simply just replay any two or three helix rift missions via the progress tracker and you will get the trophy.

Blade In The Crowd

Assassinate 100 enemies.

This trophy can come naturally as you play the story and perform assassinations with your hidden blade. To get this achievement you have to assassinate 100 enemies. This is most easily done by assassinating guards you see roaming around in the city and on the rooftops. You do not have to do this in one go, you can stop and come back later as your progress will be saved. Keep assassinating enemies and the trophy will eventually be earned.

Falling From The Sky

Perform 10 air assassinations.

This trophy can come naturally as you play the story and perform air assassinations with your hidden blade. To perform an air assassination, roam the rooftops and highlight an enemy you seen down below you roaming on the grounds then press the attack button to jump off and kill him. Keep jumping on enemies and this trophy will eventually be earned.

Know-It-All

Complete all training missions.

To get this trophy, first you need to complete at least Sequence 9 of the story and unlock all 25 skills there is to get. You can then renovate the Caf√© Th√©atre's Upper Floors for 5000 ‚Ç£, which would unlock the training room. Visit the training room and complete all of the training missions you can do and eventually you will earn this trophy after finishing all.

Gentleman Cambrioleur

Lockpick 20 chests.

This trophy can come naturally as you open all the locked chests in Paris with a red chest icon as you go towards total 100% sync. To open a locked chest, you would need the lockpick ability for 2 sync points so you can be able to open locked doors and chests. Visit at least 20 locked chests and lockpick them and you should eventually earn the trophy.

Business And Pleasure

Earn a total of 50,000 livres.

You can get this trophy naturally as you earn money by looting chests and completing missions. The quickest way to earn money is basically just recycling companion missions as these are quick, easy, and is a guaranteed money farming method. You can still spend your livres as this trophy counts for total income. You can keep recycling these money making methods and get up to 50,000 livres for the trophy!

And Stay Down!

Perform a ground execution.

To get this trophy, you can perform a ground execution at least once which is a skill you can learn for 8 synch points just after completing sequence 9. A ground execution is a fatal strike afainst a prone enemy and to perform it, you can hold the dodge button when your enemy is on the ground. Just do this once and you should hopefully earn the trophy.

Help Me!

Complete 10 Crowd Events.

You can get this trophy naturally find and explore the city to trigger at least ten crowd events. Crowd events are basically random encounters that show up as outlined icons where you can either help a random citizen or protect citizens from guards or criminals. You can continuously complete crowd events for more rewards. You can trigger at least ten crowd events and eventually you will earn the trophy.

Don't Need It

Drop 20 Money Pouches in the streets.

To get this trophy, first you can get the money pouch skill from the Skills menu for 2 synch points after completing Sequence 2. After getting the skill, you can just equip it from your inventory and drop money pouches on the street continuously whenever you feel like being nice to people. Refill at a shop when you need to and keep making those poor bastards happy and eventually you will earn the trophy.

Patron of the Arts

Watch a play in the Caf√© Th√©√¢tre.

To get this trophy, first you can complete all of the building upgrades of the Caf√© Th√©√¢tre and then complete all of the Caf√© Th√©√¢tre Missions for the manager, Mme Gouze. You can then visit the main building and start watching a play by drinking a coffee at the bar or by going near the stage. You will be watching the play when he leans on something and the game camera zooms in on the stage. Watch the whole performance and you will earn the trophy.

Share the Wealth

Get all the rewards in a Co-op mission in Paris.

To get this trophy, you can replay any co-op mission via the Progress Tracker at least twice to earn the other rewards possible. You naturally earned at least one reward when you played the mission at least once. Just replay any co-op mission twice to earn all other rewards and you will earn the trophy, hopefully.

Room With A View

Enjoy the view of Paris from Arno's balcony.

To get this trophy, you can visit Arno's room in the Caf√© Th√©√¢tre and go over to the balcony. Just lean there and enjoy the view of Paris for the trophy.

Poked!

Kill 20 enemies with a Long Weapon.

To get this trophy, you can first buy and equip any long weapon from the main menu. You can then wreck havoc on the streets and slaughter at least 20 victims and the trophy should eventually pop up.

Chopped!

Kill 20 enemies with a heavy weapon.

To get this trophy, you can first buy and equip any heavy weapon from the main menu. You can then unleash your rage on the streets and commit murder on at least 20 of your enemies and the trophy will pop up eventually.

Guillotined

Kill an enemy with a lift counterweight.

To get this trophy, you can first find a guillotine lift anywhere in Paris. These lifts will launch you into the air and on the rooftops. Attract an enemy near a lift, then use a smoke or stun bomb to make him stand underneath the lift counterweight. Use the lift and he should meet his untimely demise for your trophy. I hope you're proud of yourself.

Never Say Die

Revive a partner in Co-op.

To get this trophy, first you need to find a friend in real life that plays Assassin's Creed Unity (Impossible these days). Hop on a Unity co-op mission with your friend (if you found one), and then you can have your friend die from fall damage as in co-op mode you don't simply die, just fall on the floor. You can revive your partner from the fall damage and the trophy can be earned.

Merciful Killer

Perform 10 non-lethal takedowns in a Co-op mission.

To get this trophy, you can perform non lethal takedowns on enemies by going near an enemy without being detected and hold the interact button to perform the takedown. You can keep knocking out at least ten enemies and the trophy should eventually be eanred.

Choreography

Perform 10 Co-op sync kills.

To get this trophy, first you need to find a friend in real life that plays Assassin's Creed Unity (Impossible these days). Hop on a Unity co-op mission with your friend (if you found one), and then kill an enemy at the same time as your friend to perform a co-op sync kill. You will know you have performed a sync kill when the double sync assassination notification appears. Just keep killing fools at the same time as your friend and eventually you will earn the trophy.

The Bells! The Bells!

Sabotage 5 alarm bells in Paris.

You should naturally earn this trophy as you complete the optional challenges during the main storyline missions. To sabotage an alarm bell, you can just go near an alarm bell and press the interact button to sabotage it. You can do this during free roam as well if you find any in the streets of Paris. Sabotage any five and the trophy should pop up.

Must've Left it Open

Lockpick 5 doors.

You should naturally earn this trophy as you complete the optional challenges during the main storyline missions. To lockpick a door, you can get the ability first for two sync points then just go near a locked door and press the interact button to lockpick it. You can do this during free roam as well if you find any in the streets of Paris. Lockpick any five doors and the trophy should pop up.

I Got Skills

Unlock all Skills.

This is the most tedious trophy in AC Unity, but you can still get this trophy naturally as you keep unlocking skills to get Arno stronger and more resilient on the streets of Paris. You can unlock skills with sync points, which you can earn by completing all the single player missions, finding all 40 sync point collectibles in co-op missions, and all completing co-op heist missions. You should earn all the sync points there is to get by completing all of these missions, so just use them in the Skills section of the main menu and get every skill there is to get and you should earn the trophy. You can see here all of the Skills you can buy in the game.

Reign Of Terror

Kill 5 enemies at the same time with a Guillotine Gun.

In Dead Kings, to get this trophy, first you can complete Sequence 13 Memory 2, "The Book Thief", so you can unlock the guillotine gun. You can find at least five guards in the vicinity or attract small groups together with a cherry bomb, then equip the guillotine gun and blast at them so you can earn the trophy.

Freedom Fighter

Kill 15 Raider leaders.

In Dead KIngs, you may get this trophy naturally as you kill raider leaders throughout the game for total 100% sync. You can find groups of raiders in the underground territories below Franciade. Each group will have a leader marked with a , who you can kill first in combat so the rest of the raiders in that group will flee and panic. Just kill any fifteen of them and the trophy should eventually show up.
