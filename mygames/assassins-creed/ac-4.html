Achievements and Trophies

The following achievements and trophies can be achieved during one or multiple playthroughs of the game, or in free roam after Story Completion. These are discussed in a separate section as these are may or may not naturally happen during first playthrough of the game. Trophies and Achievements can also be obtained during free roam after completion of the main game. Do note that the Multiplayer trophies are very time-consuming so it is recommended to try for these trophies as early and soon as you can.

Hungover

Wake up in a haystack.

To get this trophy, you can visit any tavern in any region and interact with the bottle and drink it for 5 Reales. Interact with the bottle at least 5 times and you will get the trophy when Edward wakes up!

Silence, Fool!

Kill a guard ringing a bell.

To get this trophy, you can enter any restricted area with an alarm bell in the area. Make sure you are seen by an enemy and make sure he runs to ring the alarm bell, which you can tell by the alarm bell icon above his head. Once he gets to the alarm bell and starts or is about to start ringing, kill him quickly to get the trophy!

Killer Killer

Harpoon a killer whale.

You can get this trophy naturally as you go hunting and harpooning goods for crafting outfits, ammunitions and upgrades. You can find killer whales on the map in the Cabo de Cruz and Conttoyor region. Visit any of those regions, then find and complete the harpooning side activity on the killer whale for the trophy! It is recommended to get all harpooning upgrades to inflict as much damage as possible on the killer whale.

Employee Of The Month

Complete 25 Abstergo challenges.

You can get this trophy naturally as you complete Abstergo challenges. You can see the abstergo challenges you can complete easily through the Animus Main Menu, but you will naturally complete a lot of the challenges anyway as you complete the main storyline and go for total 100% Sync.

Business And Pleasure

Earn 50,000 reales.

You can get this trophy naturally as you earn money by looting chests, ship convoys, selling cargo, completing assassin contracts, Kenway's Fleet, and raiding plantations. The quickest way to earn money is basically just by plundering ship convoys as ships have lots of money and loot you can sell. You can still spend your Reales as this trophy counts for total income. You can keep recycling all of these money making methods and get up to 50,000 Reales for the trophy!

Mer-man

Swim a total of 1 nmi.

You can get this trophy naturally as you swim across the seas of the West Indies. Just keep swimming as much as you can and eventually you will get the trophy. You can track how much nmi you swimmed in the Animus Pause Menu > Animus Database > Statistics.

Redingote Up!

Craft the Hunter outfit.

To get this trophy, you will need to first get the White Jaguar skin and the Red Howler Monkey skin. You can naturally get the White Jaguar skin after the Templar Hunt Mission, "The Taino Assassin", where you can skin the animal after the mission ends in free roam. You can find the Red Howler Monkey in Isla Providencia. You can also buy both pelts from a General Store for 11K Reales total. Once you have the pelts, visit Crafting via the Animus Pause Menu and craft the outfit to get the trophy!

Thug Life

Plunder 30 ships.

You can get this trophy naturally as you plunder ships for money, cargo and resources to upgrade the Jackdaw. To plunder a ship, you have to board the ship and complete the side activity objectives to plunder it of its cargo. Target small and easy ships and keep on plundering them and assert your dominance for the trophy!

Destroyer

Fully upgrade the Jackdaw.

This is the most time consuming trophy in AC4, but you can still get this trophy naturally as you keep purchase upgrades for the Jackdaw in between main sequences and secondary activities. You will also need to find all of the elite blueprint plans to be able to get the last parts of some upgrades, which can be found by completing underwater shipwreck locations and buried chests. Ways to earn cargo and resources is just by plundering ships and raiding plantations. Ways to earn money is listed under the "Business and Pleasure" trophy. If playing on PC, there is no shame in using a trainer to upgrade the Jackdaw to the max. Just keep plundering large ships, earn money through different activities, and keep getting those upgrades until you fully turn the Jackdaw into a beast for the trophy! You can see here all of the Jackdaw's Upgrades and Costs.

Cannon fodder

Recruit 500 crew members.

This is another time consuming trophy, but you can get it naturally as you recruit crew members for the Jackdaw by saving them during fights in major locations, hiring them from taverns, saving cast away sailors, and boarding and plundering ships. Quick ways to maximize your crew is by investing in crew storage so you can carry up to 50 crew members at a time. You can also plunder bigger ships as the bigger the ship the more crew you will get. You can track your recruited crew members via the Animus Pause Menu > Animus Database > Statistics. Keep growing your pirate crew up to a total of 500 members for the trophy!

FTFY

Fully upgrade your hideout.

To get this trophy, you can upgrade the Great Inagua hideout buildings after Sequence 4 Memory 1, "This Old Cove", at icons around Great Inagua. You can upgrade the Harbourmaster, General Store, tavern, campfire, brothel, mansion facade, mansion tower and gardens and a guest house for a total of around 90K Reales. You can fully upgrade all of the Great Inagua renovations to get the trophy!

Ghost In The Machine

Hack 15 computers in Abstergo Entertainment.

To get this trophy, you can exit the Animus via the Animus Pause Menu > Quit > Exit Animus. When you exit the animus and return to present day, you can access the minimap by pressing X and use the map to find computers you can hack for the achievement. Keep in mind there are 33 total Computers you can hack, but you only need 15 for the trophy. It is best to go for this trophy after completion of the main storyline as you will have all of the clearance levels by then. You can see here where to find all computers in Abstergo Entertainment for the trophy!

Roped In

Perform 5 air assassinations from a swinging rope.

To get this trophy, you can access swinging ropes on your Jackdaw on the side of the steering wheel. To perform an air assassination from a swinging rope, jump on the rope and while you are swinging make sure there is a highlighted enemy below you then press the attack button to air assassinate off the rope. You can do these while boarding a ship, so to get the trophy quickly you can go back and forth between the Jackdaw and the ship and keep air assassinating off swinging ropes at least five times for the trophy.

Sharing Is Caring

Share each type of discovery with friends once.

To get this trophy, first keep in mind that this is an online-only achievement and you can only get it when there is a wifi symbol present in the lower left corner of the minimap, which means that you are connected to Ubisoft servers. There are three types of discoveries you can find, which are social chests, social royal convoys, and white whales. You can find these kinds of discoveries will have a light blue icon instead of white. When you get near a discovery it will be shared with friends automatically so all you need to do is just sail near one. Social Royal Convoys and White Whales can be found more easily in the seas, the social chests are more challenging as they are more rare to find. You can find a map of potential locations of these discoveries here.

All Aboard!

Board a ship without losing any crew members.

To get this trophy, you need to complete Sequence 3 Memory 3, "Prizes and Plunder" and board at least a Brig (10 enemy crew) in the open seas. Use the spyglass to locate a Brig then shoot at it until it is almost broken. Leave the Jackdaw and jump into the water. Swim to the incapacitated Brig and kill all the enemies on it, then go back to the Jackdaw and board the now empty Brig. As soon as you board the Brig, you should get the trophy!

Siren Song

Rescue pirate hostages by distracting enemies with "dancers."

To get this trophy, make sure that crew has not reached the max capacity but if so, board a ship and let the enemies kill some member of your crew. Fast travel to the tallest viewpoint in Kingston and hire a group of Dancers nearby and use them to distract the guards holding pirates at gunpoint. You will get the trophy as a result!

Wild West Indies

Kill 4 enemies in a row using multi-pistols.

To get this trophy, first you need to craft the three additional pistol holsters in order to carry a total of four pistols. You can find or buy all the pelts needed to craft the pistol holsters. Once armed with the four pistols, find a group of four or more enemies and open fire - this does not need to be done in open combat. You will get the trophy as a result!

Committed To The Cause

Reach level 55 in Multiplayer.

To get this trophy, you will need to reach the highest level in Multiplayer, which is Level 55. To reach Level 55 you will need to gather 526,000 XP total. You can earn XP by getting kills, completing matches and challenges. The quickest method to farm XP is Wolfpack mode. You can get around 7K XP per match if you complete all waves and can also get around 1K points per kill if you complete the match as fast as possible with quick stealth kills, while locked on to the enemy. You can keep farming this mode to eventually get the trophy; it will take some time and effort to do so, but one must remember that patience is key.

Personal Bag Of Tricks

Finish a game session with an ability set that you customized in Multiplayer.

To get this trophy in Multiplayer mode, you can start a match and access your Ability sets mid-match via Main Menu > Ability Sets (L1). You can choose any available ability you can access, then complete the match to earn the trophy.

Master Of The Caribbean

Complete the Discovery Mode of Wolfpack in Multiplayer.

To get this trophy in multiplayer, you can complete wolfpack discovery mode which is basically a tutorial for the wolfpack mode which consists of 8 Missions. Simply just complete these 8 Missions solo for the trophy.

Lab Technician

Play and complete a game session of Game Lab in the Multiplayer Public playlist.

To get this trophy in multiplayer, you can simply just play a public match of Game Lab from the main menu and complete the match to earn the trophy.

All Rounder

Play on every game mode, and use every ability and ranged weapon once in Multiplayer.

To get this trophy in multiplayer, you will need to first level up to 48 so you can unlock all of the abilities and ranged weapons. You have to then purchase the abilities and weapons for animus credits. Your first goal is to complete all the game modes at least once, which is Manhunt, Artifact Assault, Domination, Wanted, Deathmatch, Assassinate, Wolfpack Discovery Mode and Wolfpack. A quick way to use all of the abilities and weapons is by buying the copycat perk, so if you are killed in a public session by another player, you can choose to copy your killer's ability set and use any abilities and weapons they have bought and equipped as it will count for the trophy. Keep track of all of the abilities, weapons, and modes you have used, and eventually you will get the trophy once you have participated in everything AC4 Multiplayer has to offer.

Sacred Land

Playing as The Jaguar, be the highest scoring player of a Domination game session.

To get this trophy in multiplayer, search for a domination game session with a small amount of players and choose to play as the Jaguar if you can (if not, just quit the game and search again). You earn points in Domination by capturing and holding points; killing and stunning enemies get you more points. You can get this trophy quicker with 4 friends or people and make two clans with 2 people each, then starting a domination game session in clan matches to have better chances or coming out on top as the Jaguar for the trophy.

Queen Anne's Revenge

Playing as Blackbeard, perform an acrobatic kill and a gun kill in less than 10 seconds.

To get this trophy in multiplayer, first make sure you are a level 38 so you can get the gun ability. Enter a solo wolfpack match on Saba Island while choosing to play as Blackbeard. When the first Defend the Chest wave starts, go to chest A and hang from the wall near it. Once an enemy nears the chest kill him once he is highlighted and while your facing the wall to perform an Acrobatic kill. Quickly go to chest B and perform a gun kill on the enemy there for the trophy.

Preemptive Strike

Playing as The Orchid, block 10 abilities from opponents with Sabotage.

To get this trophy in multiplayer, first make sure you are a level 28 so you can get the sabotage ability, then go into a solo wolfpack mode while choosing to play as Orchid. During a Defend the Chests wave, enemies who loot the chest use an ability to turn invisible so use the sabotage ability before they do so when you get close to them. You will know you have done it when you hear an "eee" sound. You can do this in one single match or over more matches as this trophy is cumulative; once you use the ability on at least 10 enemies you will get the trophy.

Elevator to the Gallows

Kill a player using a lift that has been trapped with Booby Trap.

To get this trophy in Multiplayer, first make sure you are a level 26 so you can get the booby trap ability. Go into a private or public Manhunt mode match and while in the match, if you are the prey find a lift and place a booby trap on it. Use the lift and stand the top... eventually some dumb person will try to kill you using the lift to their own failure, while giving you the trophy.

Seeds of Independence

Liberate 500 slaves.

You will get this trophy in Freedom Cry naturally as you liberate slaves all around the map for upgrades. The quickest methods to liberate large amounts of slaves is raiding plantations and freeing slave ships, as you can free from 30 up to 90 slaves in one go. Keep practicing emancipation across the map and you will eventually get this trophy!

VIEWPOINTS

Carrying on from previous Assassins Creed Games, you can still synchronize with viewpoints to reveal the map and its surrounding areas. To synchronize with a viewpoint, climb the building to reach it and press the interact button upon climbing. You can perform a leap of faith off a viewpoint to easily get back to ground. Synchronizing with all viewpoints in all regions of the West Indies sea is required for 100% total sync. It is recommended to synchronize viewpoints as soon as you enter a new region to make navigation and mission / collectible finding easier.

You can see the Video Walkthroughs in the Collectibles sections to see all viewpoints in each major, secondary locations and side activity locations.

MAYAN STELA

You can start to solve Mayan Stela Puzzles after Sequence 4, Memory 1, "This Old Cove". To solve a puzzle, climb to the top of the stele upon reaching it and press to interact. Align the shape on the highlighted objects to reveal the treasure, then dig it up to find the hidden mayan stone. Collecting all 16 Mayan Stones will reveal a Mayan outfit reward you can find and collect in Tulum behind a hidden door. You can solve all 15 Mayan Stela Puzzles for total 100% Sync.

BURIED CHESTS

You can find treasure maps on explorers' cadavers. They will lead you to a specific place on an island. Dig there for buried chests and you might find money or elite plans to fully upgrade your ship (be sure to find these buried chests as early as you can to turn the Jackdaw into a monster!). You can find all 22 treasure maps and buried chests for total 100% sync.
