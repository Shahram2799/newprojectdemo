Achievements and Trophies

The following achievements and trophies can be achieved during one or multiple playthroughs of the game, or in free roam after Story Completion. These are discussed in a separate section as these are may or may not naturally happen during first playthrough of the game. Trophies and Achievements can also be obtained during free roam after completion of the main game.

Stalker killer

Counter-Kill 30 Stalkers

This trophy can come naturally as you engage in combat with stalkers and perform counter kills on them as they are about to attack you. To perform a counter kill on a stalker, walk for a stalker to come out of hiding then trigger the on-screen prompt perform the counter kill. You can find Stalkers hidden in any hiding spot, so you can use eagle vision to easily find them. New York is a great place to look for stalkers as it is a big map and most of these enemies are here. Keep grinding those counter-kills on those hooded freaks and eventually you will get the trophy.

Dedicated Employee

Complete 35 Abstergo challenges.

You can get this trophy naturally as you complete Abstergo challenges in AC Rogue. You can see the abstergo challenges you can complete easily through the Animus Main Menu, but you will naturally complete a lot of the challenges anyway as you complete the main storyline and go for total 100% Sync.

Phantom Queen

Fully upgrade the Morrigan.

This trophy can also be time-consuming like "The Destroyer" in AC4, but you can also get this trophy naturally as you keep purchase upgrades for the Morrigan in between main sequences and secondary activities. You will also need to find all of the blueprints to be able to get the best parts of some upgrades. Ways to earn cargo and resources to upgrade is just by plundering ships, raiding plantations and supply camps. You can also plunder the ships and loot chests if you are in desperate need of money. If playing on PC, there is still no shame in using a trainer to upgrade the Morrigan to the max. Just keep plundering large ships, earn money through different activities, and keep getting those upgrades until you fully turn the Morrigan into a legendary ship for the trophy! You can see here all of the Morrigan's Upgrades and Costs.

Camper

Loot 20 supply camps

This trophy can come naturally as you raid and loot supply camps at for money and resources. To loot a supply camp you need to sneak into a camp and loot the chest in the area. You can also loot big supply camps by stealing the key from a keyholder that unlocks the warehouse in the area. Keep raiding and looting supply camps and eventually you will get this trophy.

What's yours is mine

Loot 20 ship convoys

This trophy can come naturally as you board / sink and plunder ship convoys as you encounter them in the open seas. Ship convoys are typically grouped by a big ship accompanied with two smaller ships, and can be identified at or as Royal Convoys . Plundering the big ship typically counts towards the trophy, and you can either board or sink them so keep plundering those convoys and eventually you will unlock the trophy.

Do not want

Counter 20 smoke bombs successfully using a gas mask

This trophy may come naturally as you engage in combat with enemies and perform counter attacks when they throw a smoke bomb. To counter a smoke bomb using a gas mask, you can simply just use the dodge button once the smoke bomb has been launched by the enemy. You will get the gas mask during the events of Sequence 3. It is easier to replay Gang HQ memories to get this trophy because the Master Assassin will always throw a smoke bomb at you when you engage in combat with him. Keep countering those smoke bombs and eventually you will get this trophy.

Repairman

Repair all computers in Abstergo Entertainment.

To get this trophy, you can exit the Animus via the Animus Pause Menu > Quit > Exit Animus. When you exit the animus and return to present day, you can access the minimap by pressing X and use the map to find computers you can fix for the achievement. Keep in mind there are 20 total Computers you can fix. It is best to go for this trophy after completion of the main storyline as you will have all of the clearance levels by then. You can see here where to find all computers in Abstergo Entertainment for the trophy!

Globe Trotter

Complete 17 story missions in the Naval Campaign

To get this trophy, you can acquire ships and send them to join your fleet via the Naval Campaign, which you can access in the Captain's Cabin on the Morrigan. Keep in mind there are a total of 33 Story Missions to complete, but you only need 17 for the trophy. After completing all Fleet missions, you can unlock the ultimate naval campaign reward from a special location in the North Atlantic.

Smashing

Destroy 100 Ice bergs

This trophy may come naturally as you smash and break down ice bergs as you crash into them on the open seas. It is also recommended to max out the ship's icebreaker ram so you can smash icebergs faster. You can track your smashed icebergs via the Animus Pause Menu > Animus Database > Statistics. Keep sailing like a reckless sailor and crash into every iceberg you see and eventually you will get the trophy.

Ice Breaker

Break through 500 meters of Ice Sheets

This trophy may come naturally as you sail through sheets of ice across the seas of the Northern Atlantic. It is also recommended to max out the ship's icebreaker rams so you can sail through ice sheets with great speed. Keep sailing through these ice sheets and eventually you will get the trophy.

Freedom fighter

Free 300 British Prisoners of War

To get this trophy, you can find prisoner of war ships across the map marked with a . To free the prisoners of war from these ships, you have to incapacitate and board the prisoner's ship. Each of these ships carry 100 prisoners (be careful that no one dies!)... so you will need to free at least 3 of these ships for the trophy!

Unicorn Slayer

Harpoon a Narwhal

You can get this trophy naturally as you go hunting and harpooning goods for crafting outfits, ammunitions and upgrades. You can find narwhals on the map in the Fort Baie Rouge region. Visit the region, then find and complete the harpooning side activity on the narwhal for the trophy! It is recommended to get all harpooning upgrades to inflict as much damage as possible on the narwhal.

Defence First

Survive a Reverse-Boarding

You can get this trophy naturally as you complete Sequence 2, Memory 1, "One Little Victory" as you mostly only experience a reverse-boarding during this memory, although you can also experience it as bounty hunters come after the Morrigan. You can simply just replay the memory, and complete the reverse boarding activity in the memory to earn the trophy, although it is most likely you will earn it the first time you played the memory.

Denied

Counter 15 air surprise attacks

You can get this trophy naturally as stalkers attempt to air assassinate you on the streets of New York. You can counter air assassinations from enemies by countering the attack with the parry button then killing the enemy. Simply just keep countering these air assassination attempts and eventually you will get this trophy.

Ninja

Complete an Outpost without getting detected

To get this trophy, you can raid any big supply camp and complete the objectives to plunder it without getting detected by enemies. The easiest way to do this is by using berserk darts on enemies so you can watch them take each other out, and then use stealth assassinations when it permits. Loot the camp without being detected and you will earn the trophy!

Instant Vikings

Hit 5 enemies with a berserk grenade at the same time

To get this trophy, you will have to use berserk grenades on a group of at least 5 enemies. You can find large groups of enemies in cities or you make groups of small enemies gather together with firecracker darts. Once you find a group of at least 5 enemies, get high enough so you can see them altogether and quickly equip the grenade launcher and shoot at them to earn the trophy!

Nap time

Put 5 enemies to sleep with a sleep grenade at the same time

To get this trophy, you will have to use sleep grenades on a group of at least 5 enemies. You can find large groups of enemies in cities or you make groups of small enemies gather together with firecracker darts. Once you find a group of at least 5 enemies, get high enough so you can see them altogether and quickly equip the grenade launcher and shoot at them to earn the trophy! You can then shoot a berserk grenade at them afterwards to earn the "Instant Vikings" trophy!

Hunt the hunted

Sink 10 ships in North Atlantic without dying while only the HUNTED cheat is active.

To get this trophy, you will have to first unlock the Hunted cheat in the Animus Pause Menu by completing 50 Abstergo challenges. Once you have done so, activate the cheat via the Abstergo Challenges menu then wreck havoc across the North Atlantic and sink every ship you can see until you get to #10 for the trophy! It is recommended to fully upgrade the Morrigan first if you can before going for this trophy as it will be easier to sink ships with all of the weapons and armour upgrades.

I ENDURE

Sink 10 ships in North Atlantic without dying while only the ENDURANCE cheat is active.

To get this trophy, you will have to first unlock the Endurance cheat in the Animus Pause Menu by completing 55 Abstergo challenges. Once you have done so, activate the cheat via the Abstergo Challenges menu then wreck havoc across the North Atlantic and sink every ship you can see until you get to #10 for the trophy! It is recommended to fully upgrade the Morrigan first if you can before going for this trophy as it will be easier to sink ships with all of the weapons and armour upgrades.

Supplier

Take over 10 large supply camps while only the VETERANS cheat is active.

To get this trophy, you will have to first unlock the Veterans cheat in the Animus Pause Menu by completing 65 Abstergo challenges. Once you have done so, activate the cheat via the Abstergo Challenges menu then travel to supply camps in River Valley and raid and loot them. With the cheat active the enemies will be tougher and can deal a lot of damage, so your best approach to the supply camps is to go it silently and use berserk darts on enemies. Loot the chests in at least 10 large supply camps this way and eventually you will get the trophy.

Killing Machine

Kill 30 guards without dying while only the ENDURANCE cheat is active.

To get this trophy, you will have to first unlock the Endurance cheat in the Animus Pause Menu by completing 55 Abstergo challenges. Once you have done so, activate the cheat via the Abstergo Challenges menu then wreck havoc on guards in New York as the city has more enemies. The best strategy to approaching enemies is to do stealth assassinations and use berserk darts on enemies as with the cheat active, Shay's health does not respawn. Keep wrecking those hooligans with the cheat active and eventually you will get the trophy!

You can capture collectibles scattered throughout all 64 locations across the Northern Atlantic. You can collect chests to earn money. You can chase and catch music sheets to teach your crew new shanties. You can find animus fragments to complete Abstergo challenges. You can find design blueprints kept in specific chests that unlock precious items and the best ship upgrades. You can also find and collect war letters for animus database entries. Additionally, you can use eagle vision on cave paintings to understand the lore and backstory among the natives that you can read in the Animus database. Finding prosperity gems increases the money and resources that you receive from various activities. You can find all collectibles in all 64 locations for 100% total sync.

TAVERNS

Similar to Black Flag, you can also unlock locked taverns in Rogue to recruit crew members, get intel from the barkeeper and play minigames. Unlock taverns by beating up thugs; each city, village and camp has one. You can unlock all 10 locked taverns for total 100% Sync.

VIEWPOINTS

Still carrying on from previous Assassins Creed Games, you can still synchronize with viewpoints to reveal the map and its surrounding areas. To synchronize with a viewpoint, climb the building to reach it and press the interact button upon climbing. You can perform a leap of faith off a viewpoint to easily get back to ground. Synchronizing with all viewpoints in all regions of the Northern Atlantic is required for 100% total sync. It is recommended to synchronize viewpoints as soon as you enter a new region to make navigation and mission / collectible finding easier.

You can see the Video Walkthroughs in the Collectibles sections to see all viewpoints in each major, secondary locations and side activity locations.

VIKING SWORD FRAGMENTS

In AC Rogue, you can find pieces of a Viking sword scattered throughout the world. Collect all the shards to discover the location of a lost Viking treasure on Pearl Island. You can find all 15 fragments for 100% total sync.

You can see the Video Walkthroughs in the Collectibles sections to see all fragments in each major, secondary locations and side activity locations.

NATIVE TOTEMS

You can deciper the hidden code behind native totems to uncover ancient artifacts. To solve a puzzle, align all letters with the arrows on each end of the pillar. This will reveal a totem you can collect. More totems can be recovered from pillars in Native mounds and ice caves. Collecting all totems will reveal a precious Native treasure. You can solve all 7 Native Totem Puzzles for total 100% Sync.

Video Walkthrough

TEMPLAR MAPS AND RELICS

You can find templar maps that will lead you to a specific place on a specific island. Dig there for buried templar relics you can find. Collect all templar relics to reveal a precious Templar outfit reward. You can find and collect 24 Templar Maps and Templar Relics for total 100% Sync.

RENOVATIONS

Renovations are a major source of income in Assassin's Creed Rogue. You need to acquire the necessary resources before performing renovations in a city or town. You can get money and resources by looting warehouses, plundering ships, and looting chests. Plundering ships is the best and fastest method for gathering resources. You can perform all 39 Renovations for total 100% Sync.

You can see the Video Walkthroughs in the Collectibles sections to see all renovations in each major and secondary locations.

You can capture hostile settlements in AC Rogue to reveal the map of that region and all its collectibles and activities and makes renovations available for purchase. You can capture all settlements for 100% total sync.
