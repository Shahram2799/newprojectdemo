Bridge to the Turnabout

Phoenix untangles the mysteries of the past and the present to bring the Turnabout story to its climactic conclusion as he defends an all too familiar woman for the death of a mysterious children's book author against the mysterious Godot, whose secrets are finally revealed in this heart breaking finale.
